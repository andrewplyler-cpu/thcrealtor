About Your Agent

Born here and kept my roots firmly planted for good.

I'm Andrew Plyler — born in Boone, and when it came time to put down roots, there was only one place that felt like home. I came back to the High Country as soon as I could and never looked back. After twenty years in the non-profit summer camp world, I brought that same care-for-people approach to real estate. I know the back roads, the best trout streams, which ridgelines catch the sunset, and which neighborhoods actually make sense for your goals.

Whether you want a second home in Blowing Rock, an Airbnb investment in Banner Elk, raw land in Ashe County, or you're relocating from Charlotte or Atlanta — I'll walk you through every step with honest, straight-forward guidance.
